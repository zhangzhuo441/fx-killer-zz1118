The EUR/USD currency pair remains steady beneath the 1.1600 level, as traders closely monitor developments surrounding a potential US government shutdown. With the House of Representatives scheduled to vote on a stopgap funding bill, risk appetite has shown signs of improvement, yet the Euro struggles to break through this key technical threshold.

As the market awaits clarity from the Federal Reserve, the exchange rate reflects a cautious sentiment among investors. The ongoing negotiations around US funding could have significant implications for the USD, influencing broader market dynamics. For now, traders are positioned to react as the situation unfolds, keeping the focus on any potential shifts in policy that may arise from the Fed's forthcoming announcements.
